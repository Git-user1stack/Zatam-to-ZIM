How to play

Guess a 5-digit number between 10000 and 99999 in Devanagari script. You have 6 attempts and 3 lives.

🔥 Combo multiplier

Chain green hits across guesses to multiply your points up to ×5!

🌡️ Warmth bar

More green/yellow digits = hotter bar. Use it to gauge how close you are.

💀 Lives

3 hearts. Lose one when a guess has zero correct or present digits.
